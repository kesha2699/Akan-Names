Akan-Names

The Akan people of Ghana have a tradition of naming their children after the day of week they were born.These names have further meanings concerning the soul and the character of the person.The Akan name depends on the gender og the person.

The following are the Male Akan Names according to the days:

  • Sunday-Kwasi
  • Monday-Kwadwo
  • Tuesday-Kwabena
  • Wednesday-Kwaku
  • Thursday-Yaw
  • Friday-Kofi
  • Saturday-Kwame

The following are the Female Akan Names according to the days:

  • Sunday-Akosua
  • Monday-Adwoa
  • Tuesday-Abenaa
  • Wednesday-Akua
  • Thursday-Yaa
  • Friday-Afua
  • Saturday-Ama
